How it works

Sound becomes a starting map.

Personic begins with reactions to selected musical examples, then blends in personality calibration, life context, and self-reported goals. The result is not a label. It is a practical map for how to work, connect, move, date, and use music more intentionally.

01

Sound prompts capture instinctive preference: rhythm, texture, energy, tension, novelty, and emotional color.

02

Big Five calibration turns those reactions into five consumer-friendly channels: Exploration, Structure, Activation, Connection, and Emotional Sensitivity.

03

Your goals shape what happens next, so the report moves from self-insight into pointed recommendations.

PulseTempo, groove, movement, and forward drive.
TextureTimbre, density, brightness, and sonic detail.
EmotionWarmth, intensity, tension, release, and recovery.

Before automatic import exists

Spotify currently requires users to request account data manually. The report should give clear steps, let users mark the request as sent, and keep the core recommendations available while they wait.
