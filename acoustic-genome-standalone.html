The experience

From a reaction to a roadmap.

Five steps turn an instinctive response to sound into a personal roadmap you can actually use.

Step 01

Listen and respond

React to selected musical examples, then add a concise personality and lifestyle context layer so your signal can become useful guidance. You can also add listening history later to make the recommendations more specific to how you already use sound.

Step 02

Receive your Acoustic Genome profile

Each OCEAN trait gets a color family. Higher percentiles deepen that hue, creating the palette signature behind your SoundTrack Type.

Step 03

Select your growth goals

You choose the outcomes you care about. Personic compares your current profile with the Growth Direction suggested by those goals, then turns the distance into practical guidance.

Step 04

Guidance and feedback

Turn your Growth Direction into action with tools, insights, and continuous feedback across music, relationships, work, dating, and health. When listening history is added, the engine can see your real focus, energy, recovery, and social sound patterns.

Step 05

Your personal roadmap

Start with the reveal, then use your selected goals to shape a growth plan and domain-specific recommendations.

Listening history layer

Your assessment starts the map. Your real listening habits sharpen it.

SoundTrack works on its own. If you connect Spotify or upload listening history when it is available, Personic can add evidence from the music you actually return to, skip, repeat, and use throughout your day.

Optional, not required

Your standard report comes from the SoundTrack assessment, your goals, and your Acoustic Genome. Listening history is optional and can be added later when you want a sharper sound map.

Real-life signal

Spotify history can reveal listening arcs like focus music, novelty seeking, replay loops, mood regulation, workout energy, evening recovery, and social listening patterns.

Sharper recommendations

That layer strengthens Music first, then informs work focus rituals, health and recovery cues, date environments, social connection, and community matches.

No history yet

Use the assessment-only report. Add listening data later from your dashboard.

Manual bridge

Request your Spotify export, then upload it when it arrives to unlock deeper patterns.

Future connect

Once API support is live, connect top artists, genres, and listening energy more directly.
